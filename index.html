How We Work

Manufacturing Process

From concept to delivery — a disciplined process that guarantees quality at scale.

✏️
Step 01

Design Development

Our design team develops seasonal collections based on market trends, buyer briefs, and custom OEM requirements. CAD patterns and sample prototypes are prepared before production begins.

🧵
Step 02

Fabric Sourcing

We source premium fabrics from certified suppliers in Surat's textile market — cottons, rayons, georgettes, and blended fabrics tested for quality, colorfastness, and shrinkage.

✂️
Step 03

Cutting & Stitching

Precision cutting with computerised lay plans minimises wastage. Our skilled tailors and stitching units ensure consistent sizing and seam quality across every batch.

🔍
Step 04

Quality Inspection

Every garment passes through a multi-point quality check — stitch count, measurement accuracy, colour matching, and finishing standards — before moving to packaging.

📦
Step 05

Packaging

Professional packaging with custom labels, hangtags, and poly-bags tailored to buyer specifications. Export cartons are packed and marked per international shipping norms.

🌍
Step 06

Delivery & Export

On-time dispatch via road, air, and sea freight with complete documentation — Invoice, Packing List, COO, and GST/export formalities handled in-house.
